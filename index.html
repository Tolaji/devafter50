Bio

My names are Adetokunbo Olutola Osibo, am a seasoned IT infrastructure technician with years of extensive experience in deploying Computer Network infrastructures, CCTV, Wifi networks, and domain controllers. Having learned on the job, my practical expertise is underscored by a newfound passion for computer programming. Motivated by exposure to programmers who utilized my networks as a test bed for their programs, i embarked on a career change, enrolling in the Computer Programming program at BYU-I.

I am proficient in Python, HTML, CSS, and am currently expanding my skills in JavaScript and exploring the Django framework. With a keen interest in leveraging Python and it's associated libraries and frameworks for program development, i aspire to use my coding prowess to tackle real-world challenges across various industries. My long-term vision includes not only problem-solving but also the potential for substantial success in this endeavour.
